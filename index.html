CRA knowledge base

This knowledge base gives content designers a complete picture of the Canada Revenue Agency. Use it to understand the programs you are writing about, the content types CRA publishes, and how programs connect to each other.

The reference pages are lookup tools — search for a specific program, form, or credit. The conceptual sections tell the story of how CRA content is organized and how programs depend on each other. The program finder helps you quickly locate the right section.
