Folios and interpretation bulletins

Income Tax Folios are the CRA's organized technical reference documents. They present the CRA's interpretation of the Income Tax Act on specific topics and replaced the older Interpretation Bulletins.

Folio structure

Folios are organized into a three-level hierarchy:

LevelDescription
SeriesBroad subject areas (7 series)
ChapterSubdivisions within each series
FolioIndividual topic documents within each chapter

The seven series

  • S1 — Individuals
  • S2 — Employers and Employees
  • S3 — Property, Investments and Savings Plans
  • S4 — Businesses
  • S5 — International and Residency
  • S6 — Trust
  • S7 — Charities and Non-Profit Organizations (planned)

Naming convention

S[series]-F[chapter]-C[folio] — for example, S1-F1-C1 is Series 1 (Individuals), Chapter 1, Folio 1 (Medical Expense Tax Credit).

Authority level

Folios represent the CRA's general administrative positions. They are more authoritative than guides and pamphlets, but less authoritative than the Income Tax Act itself, the regulations, or court decisions. They provide useful guidance on how the CRA will typically interpret and apply the law.

Interpretation Bulletins (legacy)

Interpretation Bulletins (IT-series, e.g., IT-525R) were the predecessor to folios. They are being phased out as folios are published to replace them. Where a folio has been issued, the corresponding IT is archived. Some ITs remain active where no replacement folio exists yet.

When working on content, always check whether a folio has replaced a referenced IT.
