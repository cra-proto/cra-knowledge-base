How programs connect

CRA programs do not exist in isolation. The T1 return feeds benefit calculations. The disability tax credit unlocks a cascade of other programs. Information slips flow from employers and institutions into returns. Corporate tax integrates with personal tax through dividends. This section explains these connections so content designers can see the bigger picture.
